Your Comfort, Our Priority

Nestled amidst Mumbai’s bustling cityscape, the National Hotel is a haven of comfort located just steps away from iconic landmarks like Chor Bazaar, Girgaum Chowpatty Beach, and the historic Mohammed Ali Road. Our hotel is designed to provide a seamless experience with modern amenities, making it an ideal choice for families, solo travellers, and business guests alike.
We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service, featuring a 24-hour front desk, efficient room service, and free WiFi throughout the property. Our friendly team is dedicated to making your stay as comfortable as possible, offering services like daily housekeeping, laundry, and much more.
